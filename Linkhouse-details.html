Overview

The Link House units at Project DongKoa I offer an ideal solution for families seeking modern, comfortable living in a well-planned community. Designed for efficiency and style, these homes provide ample space with thoughtful layouts across three stories.

Owning a TH Residence Link House is an opportunity to embrace a high-quality lifestyle within Phnom Penh's rapidly developing areas, combining affordability with modern amenities and community living.
